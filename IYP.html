About

Indian Youth Parliament (IYP): Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Through Diplomacy

  • Skill Development

    At TCET-IYP, we empower individuals to develop essential life skills, including public speaking, negotiation, and research, fostering personal and professional growth.

  • Global Awareness

    TCET-IYP broadens horizons by delving into a wide array of international issues, offering participants a deeper understanding of global politics, economics, and culture.

  • Networking Opportunities

    TCET-IYP connects individuals with a diverse and valuable professional network. Participants forge meaningful relationships with peers, mentors, and experts in international affairs, opening doors to exciting opportunities.

  • Promoting Positive Change

    TCET-IYP encourages advocacy for solutions to pressing global issues, promoting international cooperation and diplomacy, and addressing the world's most urgent challenges.
